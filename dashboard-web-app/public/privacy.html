Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Orbit collects information to provide a reliable, efficient on-demand media editing and delivery platform. We collect:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, phone number, email address, and profile configurations.
  • Geolocational Data: Precise GPS coordinates (when using the delivery partner application in foreground or background) to matches shoots and assign local tasks.
  • Payment and Bank Details: Connection keys, bank account numbers, IFSC codes, and transaction histories for automated payouts.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is utilized solely to facilitate marketplace operations:

  • Matching Clients with nearby Delivery Partners for local shoot setups.
  • Processing project workflows (raw media downloads, cloud rendering, final delivery).
  • Executing bank account penny drop validation checks and routing automated payouts.
  • Notifying users via push alerts regarding task assignments, completions, and chat events.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Orbit does not sell your personal data. Information is shared strictly with payment processing integrations (Cashfree), cloud storage backends (Firebase Storage), and authorized in-house media editors assigned to your custom projects.

4. Security

We employ high-grade AES encryption, SSL transport channels, and strict server firewalls to protect database states and connected partner details.

For privacy queries, please write to us at privacy@orbitlogic.io.
